About me

I am currently a Senior Lecturer in the School of Information Technologies at the University of Sydney. Prior to that, I worked as a Lecturer at the University of Sydney from August 2017 to August 2018, a DECRA research fellow at the University of New South Wales from January 2015 to July 2017, a Postdoc at the University of New South Wales from November 2012 to December 2014, and a Postdoc at the Chinese University of Hong Kong from September 2011 to September 2012. I received my Bachelor degree in Computer Science & Technology from Renmin University of China in 2007, and my Ph.D degree in Department of Systems Engineering and Engineering Management at the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2011.

Research Interests

  • Network Science (e.g., community detection, community search, graph clustering)

  • Graph Database (e.g., subgraph matching, graph similarly search)

  • Practical Graph Algorithms (e.g., main-memory, distributed, and I/O-efficient algorithm for efficiently processing large graphs with billions of edges)
